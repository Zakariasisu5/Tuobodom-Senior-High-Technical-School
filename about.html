About the school

Tuobodom Senior High/Techical school began as community day private school on 1st January 1991.

the school started through the initiative of the committee for he Defence of the Revolution(CDR)

which was lead by Master Stephen Opuku, Mr. Kwame Effah, a member of town devolopment commitee,

Nana Osofo Yaw and Nana Fosu Aduanwoma II representing the chief of the town and all the religious

denominations in the town.

As now, the school has teaching and non-teaching staff strength of 63 and 30 respectively.

The total student population now stands at 1403 comparing 760 boys and 643 girls.

The Headmaster of the School is Mr. Charles Agyapong.
